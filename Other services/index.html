Scope and Interpolation

Interpolation :

Creating a string by combining strings and placeholder.

EXAMPLE:

Hello {{name}}!

Whatever sitting inside the scope becomes available to the view. We need to INTERPOLATE. To do that {{"double curly bracket {}"}} and whatever we put inside the curly bracket will be interpolated by angularjs. It will look at the value in the document object model in html and binds them.

$scope: Its is the glue that connects the controller data to the view.

$timeout: Its wait for a particular time and the change the value of the view.
